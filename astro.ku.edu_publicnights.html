KU Astronomy Public Nights

KU Astronomy Public Telescope Nights are every 2nd Thursday of the month just outside of Malott Hall. Weather permitting, we set out the department telescopes, including our ?” telescope to let visitors see the night sky. Join us to view planets, the Moon and other astronomical objects! Visitors are also welcome to bring their own telescope. All ages and experience welcome!

Viewing Star times:
October - March: 7:00pm
April - September: 8:00pm
